"Classical actuarial mathematics was based on deterministic approaches. How- ever, uncertainty is a fundamental characteristic of the insurance business. The number and severity of claims often appear to vary in a random manner. The study of the stochastic features of insurance business is needed to support clas- sical deterministic techniques. "
